<div>
  <div>
    Chattanooga and its surrounding areas are home to some beautiful waterfalls that are perfect for exploring. Here’s a detailed guide to these natural wonders, including locations, hours of availability, and hike lengths.
  </div>

  <div class="pb-2">
    <h2><span class="color-pink">1.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Ruby Falls</h2>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> 1720 Scenic Hwy, Chattanooga, TN 37409</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hours:</b> Open daily, hours vary by season</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hike Length:</b> Guided tour, about 1 mile round trip</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> Ruby Falls is a 145-foot underground waterfall located inside Lookout Mountain. Visitors can enjoy a guided tour that includes an elevator ride and a walk through stunning cave formations to reach the waterfall.</div>
  </div>

  <div class="pb-2">
    <h2><span class="color-pink">2.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Lula Lake Land Trust</h2>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> L5000 Lula Lake Rd, Lookout Mountain, GA 30750</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hours:</b> Open on the first and last weekends of each month, 9 AM to 5 PM</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hike Length:</b> Varies, main trail to falls is about 3.2 miles round trip</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> Lula Falls is a beautiful 120-foot waterfall within the Lula Lake Land Trust. The area features scenic trails and lush forests, making it a popular spot for hiking and picnicking.</div>
  </div>

  <div class="pb-2">
    <h2><span class="color-pink">3.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Signal Point (Julia Falls Overlook)</h2>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> Cumberland Trail, Signal Mountain, TN 37377</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hours:</b> Open daily from sunrise to sunset</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hike Length:</b> About 1 mile round trip to the overlook</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> The overlook at Signal Point provides stunning views of the seasonal Julia Falls and the Tennessee River Gorge. The hike includes some steep and rocky sections but rewards visitors with breathtaking vistas.</div>
  </div>

  <div class="pb-2">
    <h2><span class="color-pink">4.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Foster Falls</h2>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> South Cumberland State Park, Sequatchie, TN</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hours:</b> Open daily from 7 AM to sunset</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hike Length:</b> About 1.5 miles round trip to the falls</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> Foster Falls is a 60-foot waterfall that cascades into a swimming hole at its base. The moderately challenging hike features a steep descent and offers opportunities for hiking, rock climbing, and swimming.</div>
  </div>

  <div class="pb-2">
    <h2><span class="color-pink">5.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Greeter Falls</h2>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> South Cumberland State Park, Altamont, TN</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hours:</b> Open daily from sunrise to sunset</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hike Length:</b> About 1.5 miles round trip</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> Greeter Falls, a 50-foot waterfall, is accessible via a trail that includes a unique spiral staircase. The area is known for its scenic beauty and offers a swimming hole and picnic spots at the base of the falls.</div>
  </div>

  <div class="pb-2">
    <h2><span class="color-pink">6.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Savage Gulf (Suter Falls and Greeter Falls)</h2>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> South Cumberland State Park, Beersheba Springs, TN</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hours:</b> Open daily from sunrise to sunset</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hike Length:</b> Varies, trails range from short walks to longer hikes</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> Savage Gulf features multiple waterfalls, including the 30-foot Suter Falls and the 50-foot Greeter Falls. The park offers various trails that showcase the area's natural beauty and provide different levels of hiking difficulty.</div>
  </div>

  <div class="pb-2">
    <h2><span class="color-pink">7.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Cloudland Canyon State Park (Hemlock Falls and Cherokee Falls)</h2>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> 122 Cloudland Canyon Park Rd, Rising Fawn, GA 30738</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hours:</b> Open daily from 7 AM to 10 PM</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hike Length:</b> About 2 miles round trip for both falls</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> Cloudland Canyon State Park is home to Hemlock Falls and Cherokee Falls. Hemlock Falls is a 90-foot waterfall, while Cherokee Falls drops 60 feet. The trails are well-marked and include a 600-step staircase, offering beautiful views along the way.</div>
  </div>

  <div class="pb-2">
    <h2><span class="color-pink">8.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Denny Cove Falls</h2>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> 5501 TN-150, Sequatchie, TN 37374</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hours:</b> Open daily from sunrise to sunset</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hike Length:</b> About 2.8 miles round trip</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> Denny Cove Falls is a 70-foot waterfall located within South Cumberland State Park. The moderately challenging hike features scenic views and is popular among rock climbers and hikers alike.</div>
  </div>

  <div class="pb-2">
    <h2><span class="color-pink">9.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;DeSoto Falls</h2>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> DeSoto State Park, Mentone, AL</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hours:</b> Open daily from 7 AM to sunset</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hike Length:</b> Short paved trail to the upper falls, 0.5 miles round trip to the lower falls</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> DeSoto Falls is a 104-foot waterfall located in DeSoto State Park. The park offers easy access to the upper falls via a paved trail, while the lower falls require a short walk down 50 concrete stairs. The park also provides various recreational activities, including kayaking, picnicking, and hiking.</div>
  </div>

  <div class="pb-2">
    <h2><span class="color-pink">10.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Ozone Falls</h2>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> Ozone Falls State Natural Area, Rockwood, TN</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hours:</b> Open daily from sunrise to sunset</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hike Length:</b> About 0.2 miles round trip</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> Ozone Falls is a 110-foot waterfall with a short but steep trail leading to it. The area is known for its scenic beauty and features a swimming hole at the base of the falls, making it a popular spot for visitors.</div>
  </div>

  <div class="pb-2">
    <h2><span class="color-pink">11.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Twin Falls</h2>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> Rock Island State Park, Rock Island, TN</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hours:</b> Open daily from 7 AM to sunset</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hike Length:</b> About 1.5 miles round trip</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> Twin Falls is an 80-foot waterfall within Rock Island State Park. The falls can be viewed from multiple locations, and the park offers hiking, swimming, and camping. The hike to Twin Falls is relatively easy and provides beautiful views.</div>
  </div>

  <div class="pb-2">
    <h2><span class="color-pink">12.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Blowing Wind Falls</h2>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> Prentice Cooper State Forest, TN</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hours:</b> Open daily from sunrise to sunset</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Hike Length:</b> About 1.5 miles round trip</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> Blowing Wind Falls is a 30-foot waterfall accessed via the Ritchie Hollow Trail in Prentice Cooper State Forest. The moderate hike offers scenic views of the Tennessee River Gorge and the surrounding forest.</div>
  </div>

  <div style="margin-top: 3em;">Exploring the waterfalls in and around Chattanooga offers a refreshing escape into nature’s beauty. Whether you’re looking for a short, easy hike or a more challenging adventure, these stunning waterfalls provide the perfect backdrop for outdoor activities. Grab your hiking boots, leash up your dog if you’re bringing one, and enjoy the natural splendor that the Chattanooga area has to offer.</div>
