  <section class="text-2xl pb-10">
    <div>
      <h2 class="text-3xl" style="font-family: 'Libre Baskerville', serif;">Tivoli Foundation<span class="color-pink ml-1"><b>.</b></span></h2>
      <div class="pb-5">
        Composed of three venues this important Chattanooga foundation is the city staple for live performances. The restoration and preservation of these historic buildings has been and remains a priority for the city of Chattanooga, an investment valued by our residents and visitors alike. Coincidentally, at the time of last update Tivoli Theatre on Broad Street is closed for major renovations. Events are currently being held at the Memorial Auditorium or Walker Theatre.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        These venues are known for their ability to charm and captivate audiences with their architecturally styles. Each has its own character and history that immediately adds to the performance experience.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">        
        To provide a better idea of what you might find on their event schedules here is a non exhaustive idea of types performances you can attend at one of the foundations theatres.
        <ul class="text-2xl">
          <li class="pb-2">Broadway shows.</li>
          <li class="pb-2">Comedy by both established and up and coming comedians.</li>
          <li class="pb-2">Intimate and unique music performances.</li>
          <li class="pb-2">Film Series.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        <div class="pb-2"><b>Tivoli Theatre.</b> Opening in 1921 the Tivoli Theatre has been serving Chattanooga for more than 90 years. This historic Beaux-Arts style performance venue on Broad Street. You can catch a variety of performances at the 'Jewel of the South' <b>Currently closed for renovations. Tivoli events are currently being held at Memorial Auditorium.</b>.</div>
        <div class="pb-2"><b>Soldiers & Sailors Memorial Auditorium.</b> Often abbreviated to 'Memorial' this venue was dedicated to Hamilton County's armed forces in 1922 and serves as living dedication to our past, current and future service members. Host to up to 3,866 attendees Memorial is a great place for concerts, comedy and community events. Memorial Auditorium is located at 399 McCallie Ave, Chattanooga, TN 37402.</div>
        <div class="pb-2"><b>Walker Theatre.</b> Located in the same building as Memorial Auditorium, the Walker Theatre is the more intimate performance venue hosting 851 people maximum per show.</div>
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        To find their latest event offerings and reserve your spot visit the <a href="https://tivolichattanooga.com/upcoming-events" target="_blank">Tivoli Chattanooga</a> Website. 
        You can filter by venue or browse the full calendar.
      </div>
    </div>
    <div>
      <h2 class="text-3xl" style="font-family: 'Libre Baskerville', serif;">Scenic City Shakespeare<span class="color-pink ml-1"><b>.</b></span></h2>
      <div class="pb-5">
        An engaging outdoor viewing experience if there every was one.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        Come out to watch a group of passionate artists perform Shakespeare in the park at Greenway Farms. These performances are seasonal the most recent running from May-June 2025. Past playbills indicate the productions usually occur in the fall and spring of each year on Friday and Saturday evenings. Check to see when their next production will be on <a href="" taget="_blank">their website</a>.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        These performances are perfect for people who appreciate the heart, passion, and hard-work that goes into crafting a live production, enjoy smaller crowds for a less busy viewing experience, or you like different reasons to get outside for an evening.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        It is recommended audience members bring lawn chairs, blankets, and refreshments to make the most of the evening.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        Past Shakespeare productions included:
        <ul class="text-2xl">
          <li>Much Ado about Nothing</li>
          <li>A Midsummer Night's Dream</li>
          <li>The Comedy of Errors</li>
          <li>Macbeth</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        To give you a first hand account a past audience member commented 'Their Shakespeare in the Park performances bring classic theater to life in such a vibrant and beautiful way. I recently saw Macbeth, and it was nothing short of phenomenal, the acting was powerful, the staging was creative, and the entire production was captivating from start to finish.'.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        Tickets, read along scripts and more can be found <a href="https://sceniccityshakespeare.org/tickets" target="_blank">here</a>.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        <b>Time saving note:</b> Locating their outdoor stage has given some past attendees difficulty, so Scenic City Shakespeare provided a great <a href="https://sceniccityshakespeare.org/directions" target="_blank">aerial visual to help</a>.
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="mb-16 mt-16 flex flex-col justify-center items-center">
      <OptimizedImage src="/images/chattanooga_guides/performance-arts/tivoli-theatre.jpg" alt="Tivoli Theatre exterior showing the historic Beaux-Arts style facade on Broad Street" width={700} height={600} className="rounded-lg" />
      <div class="text-center mt-4 text-[20px] max-w-2xl text-gray-600">
        <em>The historic Tivoli Theatre on Broad Street, currently closed for renovations but events are being held at Memorial Auditorium.</em>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div>
      <h2 class="text-3xl" style="font-family: 'Libre Baskerville', serif;">Barking Legs Theater<span class="color-pink ml-1"><b>.</b></span></h2>
      <div class="pb-5">
        A small venue with a big energy brought to you by a variety of diverse performers and their crew members.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        Located just off Dodds Avenue in Highland Park the Barking Legs Theater describes themselves as an eclectic, creative, inclusive environment for a variety of different community artists and performers.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        This theater company is known for their wide range of intimate performances that have included contemporary dance, experimental jazz, poetry slams, singer-songwriters, film nights, and local storytelling. They don't run short on novelty.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        If want to support local art, or you enjoy seeing unique high quality performances in a welcoming environment check out a show someday soon!
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        Events run year round, the calendar gets updated regularly based on performer availability. Most of the performances are on weekend evenings, and tickets are regularly under $20. For the most updated event info, check <a href="https://www.barkinglegs.org/" target="_blank">their website</a>.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        Past events have included:
        <ul class="text-2xl">
          <li>Jazz Tuesdays with local or touring musicians</li>
          <li>The Floor is Yours: Open mic poetry and storytelling</li>
          <li>Dance and movement showcases</li>
          <li>Independent film screenings</li>
          <li>Intimate concerts</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="mb-16 mt-16 flex flex-col justify-center items-center">
      <OptimizedImage src="/images/chattanooga_guides/performance-arts/soldiers-and-sailors.jpg" alt="Soldiers and Sailors Memorial Auditorium exterior showing the historic building facade" width={700} height={600} className="rounded-lg" />
      <div class="text-center mt-4 text-[20px] max-w-2xl text-gray-600">
        <em>Soldiers & Sailors Memorial Auditorium, dedicated to Hamilton County's armed forces and hosting up to 3,866 attendees for concerts and events.</em>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div>
      <h2 class="text-3xl" style="font-family: 'Libre Baskerville', serif;">Chattanooga Theatre Centre<span class="color-pink ml-1"><b>.</b></span></h2>
      <div class="pb-5">
        100+ years strong as a mainstay of Chattanooga performing arts. The Chattanooga Theatre Centre is one of the oldest and largest community theatres in the US, running year round productions.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        They're located in North Shore, next to Coolidge Park, hosting a wide range of shows right next to the Tennessee River. At any one time you might find:
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">        
        <ul class="text-2xl">
          <li>Family friendly musicals</li>
          <li>Classic Drama</li>
          <li>Youth performances</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        They seem to put on new productions every 6-8 weeks with shows usually Thursday - Sunday, but check their <a href="https://ci.ovationtix.com/35678" target="_blank">event calendar</a> for more details and to purchase tickets.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        Past productions have included:
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        <ul class="text-2xl">
          <li>The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Night-Time</li>
          <li>Fences</li>
          <li>Matilda the Musical</li>
          <li>A Raisin in the Sun</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="pb-16">
        <b>Helpful note:</b> There's free parking onsite, but it can fill up quickly on weekends. Arriving 15–20 minutes early can be a good idea.
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="mb-16 mt-16 flex flex-col justify-center items-center">
      <OptimizedImage src="/images/chattanooga_guides/performance-arts/chattanooga-theatre-center.jpg" alt="Chattanooga Theatre Centre exterior showing the historic building facade and entrance" width={700} height={600} className="rounded-lg" />
      <div class="text-center mt-4 text-[20px] max-w-2xl text-gray-600">
        <em>The historic Chattanooga Theatre Centre, one of the oldest community theatres in the US, located in North Shore next to Coolidge Park.</em>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div>
    </div>
    <div>
      <h2 class="text-3xl" style="font-family: 'Libre Baskerville', serif;">Honorable Mentions<span class="color-pink ml-1"><b>.</b></span></h2>
      <div class="pb-5">
        If you haven't found what you came here for, or are looking for something more niche, there are a handful of other passionate production companies performing around the area:
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        <b>Back Alley Productions (Mars Theatre):</b>  A nonprofit production company in LaFayette, GA since 2012. They perform a full season of plays and musicals at the Historic Mars Theatre, performances typically fall–spring, but their dates shift every year. BAP has been voted the Best Live Performance Venue in North Georgia 2018-2020.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        <b>Signal Mountain Playhouse:</b> Trek up to Signal Mountain for a summer outdoor musical each July (typically Friday–Saturday evenings on weekends) and a winter indoor show around February at the Mountain Arts Community Center. Find out more on their <a href="https://www.facebook.com/SignalMountainPlayhouse/" target="_blank">Facebook page</a>.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        <b>Ensemble Theatre of Chattanooga:</b> Ensemble runs year round new productions every ~6–8 weeks. Past seasons included short play festivals in January–February and musical concerts in summer. More information can be found <a href="https://www.ensembletheatreofchattanooga.com/" taget="_blank">here</a>.
      </div>
      <div class="pb-5">
        <b>East Brainerd Community Theater:</b> Based out of Christ United Methodist Church on East Brainerd Road, EBCT usually stages one play and musical per year. The summer musical tends to be a big production with a large cast. Past shows have been *The Music Man*, *Pirates of Penzance*, and *Guys and Dolls*. More details found at <a href="https://ebctchatt.com" target="_blank">ebctchatt.com</a>.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

        <h3 class="text-3xl" style="font-family: 'Libre Baskerville', serif;">The Comedy Catch<span class="color-pink ml-1"><b>.</b></span></h3>
        <div class="pb-3">
          The Comedy Catch runs shows every night. Start times vary on night and show type. Check their <a href="https://www.thecomedycatch.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">website</a> for the latest details. They're located on Station Street in the Southside entertainment district.
        </div>
        <div class="pb-3">
          They have the big names you've seen come to town regularly. In recent years they've hosted the likes of:
          <ul class="text-2xl">
            <li>Kevin Nealon</li>
            <li>AJ Wilkerson</li>
            <li>Jessica Michelle Singleton</li>
            <li>Steve Byrne</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
        <div class="pb-3">
          If you enjoy supporting brand new acts, they have the longest running comedy open mic in Chattanooga every Wednesday. Also note that they charge a $5 minimum food/drink purchase per person.  
        </div>
        <div class="pb-3">
          Important note for the parents reading, some of their events are kid friendly, the events list on their website should have an indicator. 
        </div>
        <div class="pb-3">
          If you like a bigger comedy style event they are one of the yearly host's of Lookout Comedy Festival in the fall. It's a multi-day event with shows at a few unique Chattanooga venues. Check out their website <a href="https://www.lookoutcomedyfestival.com/" target="_blank">Lookout Comedy Festival</a> for more information.

        <div class="pb-3">
          Chattanooga has it's own local improv group. You can see them in a variety venues around the city and surrounding area. They may be performing after hours at the library, or the Barking Legs Theatre, or a coffee shop in Cleveland. Ticket prices vary. You can find details on future performances on <a href="https://improvchattanooga.com/shows/" target="_blank">their website</a>.
        </div>
        <div class="pb-3">
          In addition to live performances they also offer free classes regularly and can be hired for events.
