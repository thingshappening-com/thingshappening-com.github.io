  <div class="text-2xl">
    Chattanooga and its surrounding areas are a haven for antique lovers, offering a diverse array of stores that cater to both seasoned collectors and casual enthusiasts. The region boasts a rich selection of antique shops, each with its unique charm and inventory. From sprawling antique malls housing hundreds of vendors to quaint, family-run shops filled with carefully curated vintage treasures, there's something for everyone. Whether you're in search of rare collectibles, historical artifacts, or unique home decor items, Chattanooga's antique scene promises an exciting and rewarding experience. Explore these top spots and uncover the hidden gems that each store has to offer. Here's a guide to some of the best antique stores in the area:

  <div class="pb-2 text-2xl">
    <h2><span class="color-pink">1.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Dirty Jane's</h2>
    <div>
      Dirty Jane's Antiques, located at 1910 Dayton Blvd, Chattanooga, TN 37415, is a beloved destination for antique enthusiasts. Spanning a massive 24,000 square feet, the store houses over 100 vendors offering an eclectic mix of vintage, quirky, beautiful, and historical items. Visitors can find a diverse range of antiques including furniture, records, jewelry, comics, books, clothing, blankets, art, and more​​.
    </div>
    <br>
    <div>
      <b>Go Treasure Hunting For:</b>
      <li>Vintage wicker bassinets</li>
      <li>Industrial wood furniture</li>
      <li>Decorative plates</li>
      <li>Crystal balls</li>
      <li>Antique store displays and fixtures from the 1940s and ‘50s</li>
    </div>
    <br>
    <div>
      One customer, Alexis Harris, praised the store as "THE BEST antique store in all of the greater Chattanooga area!!" She highlighted the store's eclectic array of vintage finds and friendly staff, rating it a "15/10 antique store"​.
      Another reviewer, Gretchen Renlund, described it as "a fantastic place to spend some time exploring and shopping for some amazingly unique finds," noting the unmatched customer service and selection​.
      Jessica Sitton Angela mentioned it as her favorite shop during an antique weekend in Chattanooga, appreciating the fun booths and good variety of items​.
    </div>
    <br>
    <div>
      <b>Operating Hours:</b>
      Tuesday to Saturday: 10 AM - 6 PM
      Sunday: 12 PM - 6 PM
      Closed on Mondays​.
    </div>
    <br>
    <div>
      Dirty Jane's Antiques not only offers a wide range of items but also provides a vibrant and charming atmosphere that reflects its namesake's spunk and charm. The store's unique finds and the friendly, knowledgeable staff make it a must-visit for antique lovers in the Chattanooga area​. For more information, you can visit their <a href="https://www.dirtyjanesantiques.com/" target="_blank">website</a>.
    </div>
  </div>

Visitors can explore a wide range of items, including vintage furniture, retro games, Hot Wheels, seasonal home décor, collectables, artisan goods, olive oils, and vinegars​​.

      <li>Olive Oils and Vinegars</li>

    <br>
    <div>

      Another visitor, Jay Jay Burk, enjoyed the retro games and toys, although they wished for a larger selection. They appreciated the nice atmosphere and the need for more vintage games​​.
      Katrina Rice found the store wonderful with great prices and a good selection, highlighting the friendly and sweet owners​​.

    <br>
    <div>
