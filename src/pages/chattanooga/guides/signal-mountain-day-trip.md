  <div class="text-2xl">
    Signal Mountain, Tennessee, offers a unique blend of natural beauty, local culture, and stunning views. This day trip itinerary is designed to help you make the most of your visit, starting with a cozy breakfast followed by invigorating hikes through scenic landscapes. You'll also explore local art venues and enjoy a peaceful riverside lunch. Whether you're an outdoor enthusiast, an art lover, or just looking to relax, Signal Mountain has something for everyone. With its charming coffee shops, picturesque trails, cultural centers, and inviting dining spots, this guide ensures a day full of memorable experiences. Prepare to immerse yourself in the beauty of Signal Mountain, and if you have any additional recommendations, feel free to let us know.
  </div>

  <div class="pb-2 text-2xl">
    <h2><span class="color-pink">1.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Breakfast at Mayfly Coffee</h2>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> 1605 Taft Hwy, Signal Mountain, TN 37377</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Time:</b> 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> Start your day with a delicious breakfast at Mayfly Coffee. This charming spot is known for its freshly brewed coffees, breakfast sandwiches, and pastries. The atmosphere is cozy, making it a great place to relax before heading out for a day of adventure. Popular menu items include seasonal lattes, blueberry muffins, and a variety of freshly roasted coffee blends. The coffee shop is well-reviewed for its friendly service and convenient location near hiking trails.</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Operating Hours:</b> Monday to Saturday, 7:00 AM - 3:00 PM; Closed on Sunday</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Crowds:</b> Busy on weekend mornings, so weekdays might offer a quieter experience.</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Review:</b> "A cozy coffee spot bustling on a Friday morning. Delicious coffee and friendly service." - Denisse T.</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><a href="https://www.mayflycoffee.com" target="_blank">Mayfly Coffee</a></div>
  </div>

  <div class="pb-2 text-2xl">
    <h2><span class="color-pink">2.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Visit Rainbow Lake Trail</h2>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> Ohio Ave, Signal Mountain, TN 37377</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Time:</b> 9:15 AM - 11:00 AM</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> After breakfast, head to Rainbow Lake Trail for a refreshing hike. This trail is moderate in difficulty and offers beautiful views of the surrounding forests and the historic dam. The hike to the lake is about 2.3 miles round trip, featuring rocky and challenging sections that reward you with the serene Rainbow Lake and Middle Creek. The trailhead is easily accessible and well-marked.</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Geology and Ecology:</b> The trail runs through typical Tennessee River Gorge geology with diverse plant life, including hemlocks and laurels.</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Best Times to Visit:</b> Early mornings or weekdays to avoid crowds.</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Operating Hours:</b> Daily, sunrise to sunset.</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Review:</b> "The trail is absolutely beautiful with stunning views of the falls and the lake. Perfect for a family outing."</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><a href="https://outdoorchattanooga.com/rainbow-lake" target="_blank">Outdoor Chattanooga</a>, <a href="https://www.visitchattanooga.com/listing/rainbow-lake-trail/2160/" target="_blank">Visit Chattanooga</a></div>

  <div class="pb-2 text-2xl">
    <h2><span class="color-pink">3.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Mountain Arts Community Center</h2>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> 809 Kentucky Ave, Signal Mountain, TN 37377</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Time:</b> 11:30 AM - 1:30 PM</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> Spend the afternoon exploring some local culture at the Mountain Arts Community Center. This venue offers various art exhibits, classes, and performances. The center is housed in a historic school building, providing a charming backdrop for its artistic endeavors. Local shops nearby also offer unique finds and souvenirs.</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Operating Hours:</b> Varies by event and class schedule; generally open during daytime hours.</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Crowds:</b> Generally light, but can get busy during special events and performances.</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Review:</b> "A wonderful place to experience local art and culture. The historic building adds a lot of character."</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><a href="https://signalmountaintn.gov/community/mountain-arts-community-center" target="_blank">Mountain Arts Community Center</a></div>

    <h2><span class="color-pink">4.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Lunch at River Drifters</h2>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> 1925 Suck Creek Rd, Chattanooga, TN 37405</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Time:</b> 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> Head to River Drifters for a relaxing lunch by the water. This riverside spot offers a variety of dishes, from burgers and sandwiches to fresh seafood. The serene view of the river enhances the dining experience. Popular menu items include the fried catfish, shrimp po' boy, and their house-made burgers.</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Operating Hours:</b> Wednesday to Sunday, 11:00 AM - 8:00 PM; Closed Monday and Tuesday</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Crowds:</b> Can get busy during lunch hours, especially on weekends.</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Review:</b> "Great food with an even better view. The perfect place for a relaxing lunch."</div>
    <div class="mb-0p5"><a href="https://www.facebook.com/riverdrifterschattanooga/" target="_blank">River Drifters Facebook Page</a></div>

<div class="pb-2 text-2xl">
  <h2><span class="color-pink">5.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Visit Signal Point</h2>
  <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> 116 Signal Point Rd, Signal Mountain, TN 37377</div>
  <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Time:</b> 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM</div>
  <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> After lunch, visit Signal Point for a stunning panoramic view of the Tennessee River Gorge. This historic site is also the starting point for the Cumberland Trail, offering additional hiking opportunities if time allows. The lookout provides breathtaking views and is a great spot for photography.</div>
  <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Operating Hours:</b> Daily, sunrise to sunset</div>
  <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Crowds:</b> Moderate, with more visitors during weekends and holidays.</div>
  <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Review:</b> "The views from Signal Point are incredible. A must-visit for anyone in the area." - John D.</div>
  <div class="mb-0p5"><a href="https://www.nps.gov/places/signal-point.htm" target="_blank">NPS - Signal Point</a></div>
</div>

<div class="pb-2 text-2xl">
  <h2><span class="color-pink">6.</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;Grab a Beer and Dinner at Monkey Town Brewing Company</h2>
  <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Location:</b> 287 1st Ave, Dayton, TN 37321</div>
  <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Time:</b> 5:30 PM - 7:00 PM</div>
  <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Description:</b> Conclude your day with a visit to Monkey Town Brewing Company in nearby Dayton, TN. Enjoy a craft beer from their wide selection brewed on-site. The brewery also offers a variety of food options including burgers, sandwiches, wings, and more, making it a great place to have dinner as well.</div>
  <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Operating Hours:</b></div>
  <ul class="mb-0p5 text-2xl">
    <li><b>Monday to Thursday:</b> 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM</li>
    <li><b>Friday and Saturday:</b> 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM</li>
    <li><b>Sunday:</b> 11:00 AM - 8:00 PM</li>
  </ul>
  <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Crowds:</b> The brewery can get busy during dinner hours and weekends, so arriving early is recommended.</div>
  <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Review:</b> "Service is top-notch and the food is even better. Super great atmosphere and a good time for sure. I recommend!!" - Hunter Ziegler</div>
  <div class="mb-0p5"><b>Review:</b> "Phenomenal place to gather for all occasions; from hanging out with a friend to a large group gathering. Wonderful food, reasonable prices, and excellent micro-brewed beer." - Jeff Banks</div>
  <div class="mb-0p5"><a href="https://monkeytownbrewing.com" target="_blank">Monkey Town Brewing Company</a></div>
